POKE MON

A black Charizard, or rather a Shiny Charizard, is a very rare Pokémon to obtain. Your best bet is to obtain a Shiny Charm (usually by completing the Pokédex) and breed a Charmander using the Masuda Method (at least one of the Pokémon being bred comes from a foreign country, like a Japanese Ditto). This will give you the best odds of obtaining a Shiny Charmander, which will evolve into the Charizard you so desire.
